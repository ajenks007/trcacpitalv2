Class A Office, Houston CBD

1001 McKinney

Houston, TX

TRC Capital Partners owns and manages the 23-story, 375,440-square-foot building at Main and McKinney in the Houston CBD. The Art Deco style building was designed by Alfred C. Finn and built in 1947 to house City National Bank. In 2020, TRC completed a historic renovation of the main lobby, upper floor lobbies and bathrooms, and modernized all elevators. Key amenities include a tenant lounge, fitness center, conference facility, tunnel access, and attached parking garage.
